01 — Player Profile

The Champion from Down Under

Brendan "Bren" Seve is one of the best Trackmania players in the world. Born on December 23, 1998 in France, he grew up with the game and now lives in Australia — his passion for high-speed racing has taken him to the world stage.

In 2023, he crowned his career with the Trackmania World Championship title, which he claimed together with his teammate Otaaaq under the Karmine Corp banner. In the Grand Final, he defeated legends like five-time world champion CarlJr. and broke three world records in a single match.

Beyond competitive play, Bren is famous for his epic Deep Dip streams, where he conquered Trackmania's hardest maps live in front of thousands of viewers.

03 — Legend

Deep Dip
Back-to-back

The Hardest Climb

Deep Dip is a 2000-metre tower in Trackmania — no checkpoints, one mistake sends you all the way back to the bottom. The community calls it "impossible." Bren calls it conquered — twice.

During Deep Dip 1 he streamed for 40 hours straight on Twitch, in front of over 10,000 viewers. At one point he fell from 97% all the way back to zero. He started over. He won. The community exploded.

Deep Dip 2 repeated the feat — the biggest single prize pool of his career at over $16,000, cementing his name in Trackmania history forever.
